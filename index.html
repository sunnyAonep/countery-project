to Bhutan

Bhutan is a landlocked Southern Asian country, which is situated in the Eastern Himalayas, between China in the north and India in the south.

Bhutan is known locally as "Druk Yul" or "Land of the Thunder Dragon"

Bhutan is a constitutional monarchy with a king (Druk Gyalpo) as the head of state and a prime minister as the head of government.
